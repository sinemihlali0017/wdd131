Sweet Crumbs Bakery Site Plan

Site Name

Sweet Crumbs Bakery

Site Purpose

Showcase bakery products, display photos and prices, provide location info, and allow customers to contact or place orders.

Scenarios

  • What baked goods are available?
  • How can I order a custom cake?

Color Schema

  • Brown (#8b4513) headings and buttons
  • Cream (#fffaf3) background
  • Peach (#f4b183) highlights and buttons
  • Dark Gray (#333) body text

Typography

  • Body: Lato
