Our Story

At Sweet Crumbs Bakery, we believe every day deserves a little sweetness. Founded in 2010, our Queenstown bakery has been serving the community with handcrafted baked goods made from traditional recipes and the highest quality ingredients.

From our signature chocolate cakes to our flaky croissants, every item is baked with passion and attention to detail by our skilled bakers.
